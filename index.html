WE ARE CAVALIERS

DEFENDING THE LAND. HONOURING THE PAST.

The Cleveland Cavaliers (Cavs) are an NBA team known for periods of resilience and triumph. Based in Cleveland, Ohio, the franchise's defining moment was the 2016 NBA Championship, led by native LeBron James. This historic victory, achieved by overcoming a 3-1 Finals deficit, ended the city's 52-year professional sports drought. Today, the "Wine & Gold" maintain a strong identity built around a dynamic core, including All-Star Donovan Mitchell. They continue to honor their legacy while competing fiercely in the Eastern Conference from their home at Rocket Mortgage FieldHouse. The Cavs faithful remain dedicated, always hoping for the pursuit of the next title.
